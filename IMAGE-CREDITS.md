## Replacing Images

### For Professional Photography:

When you're ready to add your own images:

1. **Hire a professional photographer** familiar with:
   - Hospitality photography
   - Natural light techniques
   - Nordic aesthetic

2. **Key shots to capture:**
   - Golden hour exterior shots
   - Room interiors with natural light
   - Brewery process shots
   - Food and beverage styling
   - Landscape and surroundings
   - Guest experiences (with permission)

3. **Technical specifications:**
   - Minimum resolution: 1920x1080px
   - Format: JPG (for photos), WebP (for web optimization)
   - Color profile: sRGB
   - Compression: Balanced quality/file size

### DIY Photography Tips:

If shooting yourself:

üì± **Equipment:**
- Modern smartphone (iPhone 12+, Samsung S21+)
- Tripod for stability
- Natural light is your best friend

‚è∞ **Timing:**
- Shoot during "golden hour" (early morning/late afternoon)
- Overcast days = soft, diffused light (perfect for interiors)
- Avoid harsh midday sun

üìê **Composition:**
- Use rule of thirds
- Keep spaces tidy and minimal
- Include natural elements (plants, flowers, wood)
- Show scale (include a person or familiar object)

üé® **Styling:**
- Keep it authentic - don't over-stage
- Add fresh flowers/herbs
- Include local products (beer, food)
- Natural materials in frame (linen, wood, ceramics)

## Image Optimization

Before uploading your own images:

1. **Resize for web:**
   - Hero images: 1920x1080px
   - Room cards: 800x600px
   - Thumbnails: 400x300px

2. **Compress without losing quality:**
   - Use [TinyPNG](https://tinypng.com)
   - Or [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app)
   - Aim for <200KB per image

3. **Modern formats:**
   - Convert to WebP for better compression
   - Keep JPG as fallback

4. **Lazy loading:**
   - Already implemented in the code
   - Images load as users scroll
