The brewing process

Ingredients

We use local ingredients where possible — malt from Danish malt houses, hops from Northern European farms, and water from Bornholm's limestone ground.

Brewing

Small batches are brewed in our small brewery on the farm. Each batch gets time to develop — no rush.

Fermentation

The beers ferment in quiet surroundings. We let nature do its work, while we keep an eye on the process.

Maturation

After fermentation, the beers get time to mature. Some mature for weeks, others for months — depending on the type.

No shows — just good craftsmanship

We don't brew to impress or to put on big shows. We brew because we love the process, the ingredients, and creating something with our hands.

ØLIV Brew is part of farm life — not a tourist attraction. It's beer brewed with respect for tradition, but with an eye for quality and taste.

When you taste our beer, you taste the place. The sea, the light, the rhythm of the island — it's all there in the glass.
