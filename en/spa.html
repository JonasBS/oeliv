Spa & Experiences

A small farm sauna, cold water and quiet surroundings — a simple ritual without spa music and grand gestures.

Farm sauna & cold water

Land after a day by the sea, warm through and feel the peace.

We light the sauna at selected times — combine with cold water, a glass of beer or just complete peace. It's a place where the pace slows down, and where you can feel the difference from city life.

The sauna is small and intimate — few guests at a time. It's about being present, not about reaching all treatments.

  • Farm sauna (few guests at a time)
  • Cold water & fresh air
  • Add-on for selected stays
  • Towels and bathrobes
  • Quiet surroundings

The ritual

1

Warm up

Enter the sauna and let the heat penetrate. We light it at selected times — usually in the afternoon and evening.

2

Cool down

After the sauna, cool down with cold water or fresh air. Jump in the sea if you dare, or use our cold water bath.

3

Relax

Take a break. Drink a glass of water, a glass of beer from the brewery, or just sit quietly and feel the peace.

4

Repeat

Repeat the cycle as many times as you want. It's about finding your own rhythm.

When is the sauna open?

We light it at selected times, usually in the afternoon (3-5 PM) and evening (7-9 PM). Contact us when booking to hear the exact times.

What should I bring?

We provide towels and bathrobes. Just bring yourself and time to relax.

Is it included?

Spa can be added to selected stays. Contact us to hear about spa packages and prices.

Can children participate?

Yes, children are welcome, but we recommend they are over 8 years old and always supervised by adults.

Other experiences

Farm tours

Get a tour of the farm. See the chickens, the garden, and learn about farm life. Free for all guests.

Coastal walks

We can recommend the best trails and places along the coast. Get our guide to the area upon arrival.

Brewery tasting

Taste beer from ØLIV Brew. Learn about the brewing process and taste different types. See the brewery page for more info.

Breakfast on the terrace

Enjoy breakfast on the terrace with sea views. Served every morning from 8-10 AM.
