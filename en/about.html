About ØLIV

Farm hospitality on Bornholm – peaceful farm stays 300 m from the coast.

The story of ØLIV

ØLIV is a small farm on Bornholm that has become a place for peace, nature and authentic experiences. We started with a vision to create something different from the traditional beach hotels – something more grounded, peaceful and honest.

The farm has been in the family for generations, and we have gradually transformed it into a place where guests can experience farm life, nature and the unique atmosphere of Bornholm.

ØLIV Brew started as a passion and is now a natural part of the stay. We brew small batches with focus on quality and taste – not quantity or shows.

Our values

Presence

We believe in being present – both for ourselves and for our guests. It's about slowing down the pace and feeling the moment.

Nature

Nature is part of everything we do. From local ingredients to sustainable operation – we respect and protect nature.

Craftsmanship

Good craftsmanship takes time. We choose quality over quantity, and we are proud of what we create with our hands.

Peace

Peace is not lack of activity – it's an awareness of time and space. We create a place where peace can be found.

Sustainability

Solar panels on the roof, low consumption, local ingredients. We strive to be part of nature, not against it.

Honesty

No shows, no exaggerations. We are honest about who we are and what we offer – and that's enough.

How to find us

From Copenhagen

Ferry from Køge to Rønne (approx. 5 hours), then 15 minutes by car or bike to ØLIV. Ferry departures several times daily.

From Ystad (Sweden)

Ferry from Ystad to Rønne (approx. 1 hour), then 15 minutes by car or bike. Faster alternative if you're coming from Sweden.

By car

Parking on the farm. We recommend coming by car to explore the island. Bornholm is perfect for road trips.

By bike

Bornholm is perfect for cycling. We have bike parking and can recommend the best routes. Many guests cycle around the island.

Sustainability

Solar panels on the roof, low consumption, local ingredients.

We strive to be part of nature, not against it. This means:

  • Solar panels cover most of our energy consumption
  • Local and organic ingredients in breakfast
  • Minimal waste – we compost and recycle
  • Water-saving solutions in all rooms
  • Natural cleaning products
  • Support for local producers and craftspeople

Frequently asked questions

What's included in the price?

The price includes accommodation, breakfast with local ingredients, and access to the farm and terrace. Spa and brewery tasting can be added.

Can I bring children?

Yes, we are child-friendly. Some rooms are better suited for families - contact us to find the right room.

What about allergies and diets?

We can adapt breakfast to allergies and diets. Tell us in the booking inquiry, and we'll take care of it.

When are check-in and check-out?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM, and check-out is by 11:00 AM. We can be flexible - contact us if you have other needs.

Is there WiFi?

Yes, there is free WiFi throughout the farm. But we encourage you to turn off your phone and enjoy nature.

Can I buy beer to take home?

Yes, we sell our beer to guests. Contact us upon arrival to hear about availability.

What if it rains?

Bornholm has its own climate, and rain can occur. We have indoor areas, and many guests enjoy the peaceful rainy days on the farm.
